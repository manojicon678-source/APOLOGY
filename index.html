Why Should You Forgive Me?

What I Did

I chose silence instead of honesty. Looking back, I realize that hiding the truth only made things worse.

Why I Hid It

Not because I wanted to hurt you, but because I was afraid. Fear isn't an excuse, and I understand that now.

What I Learned

Trust isn't broken in one second— it's broken by one wrong decision. I've learned that honesty should never depend on convenience.

What You Mean To Me

Our friendship has always meant more to me than this mistake. That's why losing your trust hurts so much.

My Promise

I can't promise to never make mistakes again. But I can promise that I'll never hide the truth from you again.

One Last Thing...

I'm not asking you to forgive me today. I'm only asking you to know that every word here is honest. If one day you decide to trust me again, I'll spend that chance proving your decision wasn't wrong.
